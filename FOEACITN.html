What's Next?

The NFL mandatory minicamps take place during the first three weeks of June. Rookie minicamps vary but typically last two-to-three days during May. Here, the former Owls will have a chance to compete for the 53-man rosters before teams make cuts Aug. 30.

As for the Owls, they will continue summer workouts before opening their season against Akron on Sept. 2 at Lincoln Financial Field.
